Privacy Policy

How we protect your personal information

1. Information We Collect

Metro Beat Events collects information you provide directly to us, such as when you fill out a contact form, request a quote, or book our services. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and event details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide quotes
  • Process and fulfill your event bookings
  • Send you updates about your event
  • Improve our services and website
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information with service providers who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business, subject to confidentiality agreements.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. To exercise these rights, please contact us at info@metrobeatevents.com or call 703-646-0293.

6. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
